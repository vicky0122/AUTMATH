Fractions

Fractions are represented as a numerical value, which defines a part of a whole.
A fraction can be a portion or section of any quantity out of a whole, where the whole can be any number, a specific value, or a thing.
The following figure shows a pizza that is divided into 8 equal parts.

If we select 2 parts of the pizza, it will be expressed as 2/8.
Similarly, if we are referring to 6 parts of this pizza, we would write it as 6/8 as a fraction.
